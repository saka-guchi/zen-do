# Refund Policy

## General Policy
Due to the nature of digital products, **all sales are generally final, and no refunds will be provided once a license key has been issued or activated.**

## Exceptions for Refund
We may, at our sole discretion, offer a refund within **14 days of purchase** in the following exceptional circumstances:
- **Technical Failure:** The Software does not function at all due to a technical issue on your system, and our support team is unable to resolve the issue.
- **Duplicate Purchase:** You accidentally purchased the same Software license multiple times.

## How to Request a Refund
To request a refund under the exceptions listed above, please follow these steps:
1. Contact us at [zendo.development@gmail.com](mailto:zendo.development@gmail.com).
2. Include your **Order Number** in the email.
3. Provide a clear and detailed **reason** for your refund request. If requesting a refund due to a technical failure, please include details about the issue and any previous communication with our support team.

## Important Notes
- Please note that depending on your payment method, any processing fees charged by the payment platform may not be refundable.
- Once a refund is processed, your license key will be deactivated, and you will no longer have the right to use the Software.
